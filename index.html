"Every patient deserves the time to be truly heard."

Suwapiyasa Family Care was built on a simple belief - that quality healthcare should feel personal. Dr. Malika Mendis brings over two decades of clinical experience to every consultation, combining precision with genuine warmth.

We are a small clinic by design. Fewer patients, deeper care. Whether you're managing a long-term condition or need a trusted doctor for your family's everyday health, we treat you as a person - not a file number.
